About S.V.S

S.V.S English medium nursery and higher primary School Innanje was the dream venture of His Holiness Sri Vishwothama Theertha Swamiji. His vision was to empower the weaker sections of society through education, in annenvironment that is stimulating, challenging and grounded in values. Every child passing out of this school grows up to be commited citizen at this great country. The School aims to bring about all round development of children and in turn prepare them to meet the ever changing challenging of a globalised world.

Sri Vishnumurthy Hayavadana Swami English Medium Nursery And Higher Primary School Innanje was founded in 2010. The beautiful lush green surroundings of the area inspired H.H Sri Vishwothama Theertha Swamiji of Sri Sode Vadiraja Mutt to open an English Medium Nursery and Higher Primary School Innanje. Mrs. Prabhavathi S Adiga was appointed as the Administrative officer, Mr. Pundarikaksha Mithanthaya took charge as the first Headmaster of this institution. In 2019 Mrs. Mamatha A was appointed as the Headmistress. In 2022 Mr. Anantha Moodithya was given the respensibility of managing the school as the new Administrative Officer.
